How to use this with app owners: During your interviews over the next 2 days, ask each app owner what stack category they are. After 70 meetings, enter the actual counts above. The model recalculates your achievable portfolio RF and shows whether you have headroom or a gap. If you have a gap, you know exactly which stack tier needs to have its per-app target raised — or you identify more apps that could be moved to "Modern" with investment.

For the leaderboard: Vendor/iSeries apps show a 🔒 badge next to their RF field with the label "RF Excluded — Vendor/Fixed App." Their DPI score card recalculates the 4 remaining pillars to sum to 100 points, so their total score is still comparable to other apps.
